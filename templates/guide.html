About

Your business needs to be using data as a competitive edge. Financial, customer, transactional, market and other data sources can be leveraged to increase profits as high as 35% annualy. Data can reveal insights such as seasonal customer purchasing patterns, specific customer demographic information, or even profit margin predictions as accurate as 90-95%. While the metrics used in this web application are important, the real power of data analytics lies within machine learning, a sub-branch of Artifical Intelligence. With machine learning, you can use advanced statistical methods to predict numbers (regression) or labels (classification). Machine Learning examples include predicting housing prices through the K-Nearest-Neighbors algorithm, calculating the probalility that a person will pay back their loan through logistic classification, or identifying the biggest factors causing a company's rising costs through a mutiple linear regression model. By utilizing your data properly, your company will be using the same competitive edge as industry leaders everywhere that include Google, Facebook, Amazon, Netflix, and Apple.
